No learning curve

Three steps. That's it.

Step 1

Forward a conversation

Copy a WhatsApp chat, record a voice note after a call, or send a screenshot. Forward it to your Rethink CRM bot on Telegram.

Step 2

AI extracts deal context

Claude AI reads the conversation and pulls out contact name, company, deal stage, next action, and sentiment. Automatically.

Step 3

Pipeline updates itself

Your dashboard reflects the deal instantly. No forms. No pipeline to update manually. The conversation is the record.

Built for how founders actually work

Changes behavior, not just workflow.

Never miss a follow-up

The bot tracks inactivity per deal stage. When a deal goes cold, you get a nudge — with full context on what was last discussed.

"Arjun @ TechCorp hasn't moved in 3 days. Last: Proposal Sent."

Pre-call context in seconds

Before any call, type /context name and instantly see: last discussion, stage, budget signal, and suggested talking points.

"Priya @ Razorpay — 4d. Budget: ₹8L. Ask about Q2 timeline."

Ask your pipeline anything

No spreadsheets. Ask in plain language using /ask and AI answers from your actual deal data.

"/ask who should I follow up today?"
