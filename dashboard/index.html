How to Capture Deals

💬
Forward a WhatsApp conversation
Copy any WhatsApp chat and forward it to the bot. AI will extract the contact, company, stage, and next action automatically.
🎙️
Send a voice note after a call
Record a quick voice memo right after a call. The bot transcribes it and extracts deal context — even from Hinglish.
📸
Send a screenshot
LinkedIn DMs, business cards, WhatsApp contact info — send the screenshot and AI reads it.

Bot Commands

/pipeline See your full pipeline grouped by stage with heat scores
/context [name] Get a pre-call brief — last discussion, stage, budget signal, talking points
/ask [question] Ask anything about your pipeline in plain language
/addnote Add a note to an existing deal (guided flow)
/note [text] Quick note to your most recently updated deal
/won [name] Mark a deal as won
/lost [name] Mark a deal as lost
/addcontact Add a contact manually (guided step-by-step)
/help See all available commands
